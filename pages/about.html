About Plumcert

Gas Safe registered CP12 inspections, boiler servicing and plumbing checks across Hertfordshire. Part of the Utilities Combined Group.

Who We Are

Plumcert is the gas safety and plumbing division of Utilities Combined Group, dedicated to providing CP12 certificates, boiler servicing and plumbing inspections across Hertfordshire.

Our team of Gas Safe registered engineers has collectively completed over 2,400 inspections for homeowners, landlords, estate agents and home buyers throughout the county.

Our Approach

We believe gas safety should be straightforward and stress-free. That is why we have built our service around three principles:

  • Clarity — Every CP12 and report is written in plain language so you understand exactly what has been found and what needs attention.
  • Speed — We know your time matters. Most inspections take 30 to 90 minutes, with certificates delivered within 24 hours.
  • Compliance — Every inspection meets the Gas Safety (Installation and Use) Regulations 1998, giving you a certificate that insurers, solicitors and letting agents trust.

Our Certifications

We are proud to hold the following accreditations:

  • Gas Safe Registered — The legally required register for any engineer working on gas appliances in the UK.
  • GSIUR 1998 Compliant — All work and reporting conforms to the Gas Safety (Installation and Use) Regulations.
  • Fully Insured — Professional indemnity and public liability insurance for every job.

Utilities Combined Group

Plumcert operates under the Utilities Combined Group umbrella, which provides a range of utility services across the UK including our sister brand ElectriCert for electrical safety inspections. This backing gives us the resources, training infrastructure and quality assurance systems that independent operators simply cannot match.
