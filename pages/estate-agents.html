Estate Agent Partnerships

Speed up your property transactions with fast, reliable gas safety compliance.

Why Partner With Us?

Missing or outdated CP12 gas safety certificates are one of the most common causes of delays in lettings and property sales. By partnering with Plumcert, your agency can offer clients a fast track to gas safety compliance, keeping sales and lettings moving.

What You Get

  • Priority Booking — Your clients get first availability. Most inspections are booked within 48 hours of referral.
  • 24-Hour CP12 Certificates — Gas safety records delivered within 24 hours of inspection, ready for tenants, solicitors and conveyancers.
  • Dedicated Account Manager — A single point of contact for your agency. No call centres, no runaround.
  • Competitive Referral Pricing — Volume-based pricing for agencies referring multiple properties per month.
  • White-Label Options — Certificates can be co-branded with your agency details for a seamless client experience.
  • Compliance Support — We help your team understand the Gas Safety (Installation and Use) Regulations 1998 so you can advise landlords confidently.

How It Works

Getting started is simple:

  • Step 1: Register your agency by completing the form below or calling our partnerships team.
  • Step 2: Receive your agency account login and referral process details.
  • Step 3: Refer clients directly. We handle booking, inspection and the CP12.
  • Step 4: Certificates are delivered to you and your client within 24 hours.

Common Scenarios We Help With

  • Annual CP12 renewals for managed rental portfolios
  • Pre-tenancy gas safety checks before new tenants move in
  • Boiler servicing to keep manufacturer warranties valid
  • Pre-purchase gas inspections requested by buyers or solicitors
  • Remedial gas or plumbing work identification before a property goes on the market
